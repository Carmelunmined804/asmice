## What is asmice?

Asmice helps you monitor speech in environments where clear instructions matter. It watches live audio feeds to track keywords and detect emotional tones. The software uses advanced models to ensure that critical messages reach the right people without misunderstanding. 

This tool serves teams that rely on precise communication. You can track speech patterns and ensure that every instruction matches your quality standards.

## 📈 Analyzing speech results

The main window displays a visual representation of sound waves. It highlights specific moments when the software identifies a command or a keyword. If the software notices a change in tone that might indicate stress or frustration, it flags that segment for your review.

You can adjust these thresholds in the Settings menu. Lower the sensitivity if the software flags too many items. Increase the sensitivity if the software misses key phrases.

## 🧩 Understanding the monitoring process

Asmice breaks down sounds into small pieces. It compares these pieces against a database of known speech markers. 

* Keyword spotting: The software listens for specific words you define in your settings.
* Emotion recognition: The software evaluates the rhythm and pitch of the voice to suggest the emotional state of the speaker.
* Clarity check: The software checks if the audio quality is high enough for accurate transcription.

## 🔐 Privacy and Data

All processing happens on your local machine. The software does not send your voice data to the internet. Your logs remain private on your computer. You control who sees your data.
